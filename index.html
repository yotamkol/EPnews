☆ Outcomes of Functional Substrate Mapping Based Ablation in Addition to Pulmonary Vein Isolation in Atrial Fibrillation
PACE · Mar 27, 2026 💬 0
# Plain-Language Summary Researchers found that patients with atrial fibrillation who had slow conduction zones in their hearts were more likely to have arrhythmia recurrence even after ablation treatment, suggesting these zones represent important targets for the procedure. Adding ablation of these slow conduction zones to standard pulmonary vein isolation improved outcomes, though patients with these zones still had lower success rates overall.
