J Cardiovasc EP · Apr 15, 2026 💬 0
# Plain-Language Summary A study of over 3,400 patients found that catheter ablation successfully eliminated atrial fibrillation symptoms in about 65% of patients at 1 year, though this declined to 52% by 42 months of follow-up. # Plain Language Summary A study of 88 patients undergoing a heart rhythm procedure (pulsed field ablation) found that while kidney function generally recovered within 48 hours, about 10% of patients experienced acute kidney injury—a higher rate than previously thought, with some cases only detectable at 48 hours rather than 24 hours.
