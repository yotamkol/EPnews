☆ Cardiac resynchronization therapy with or without atrioventricular node ablation in atrial fibrillation: the CAAN-AF trial
European Heart Journal · Apr 16, 2026 💬 0
This multicenter RCT (n=143) comparing atrioventricular node ablation (AVNA) versus medical rate control therapy (MRCT) in HFrEF patients with permanent AF receiving CRT-D found no significant difference in the primary composite endpoint of all-cause mortality and nonfatal heart failure events (IRR 1.16, 95% CI 0.60-2.24), nor in secondary outcomes including cardiovascular mortality, hospitalizations, or functional capacity. The trial was terminated early for futility, providing no evidence that AVNA enhances CRT efficacy in this population compared to optimized medical rate control.
